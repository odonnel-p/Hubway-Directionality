Hubway Bike Sharing Directionality of Trips

Boston's bike share program, the Hubway, logs thousands of trips across the city each year. This interactive map explores the published data on local bike share trips for the year 2014. First, click on the station (each black dot is a bike station). Then, a bar chart will compare the amount of trips to and from that station. The general directionality of the 10 most destinations or starting stationsis plotted around the dots.
